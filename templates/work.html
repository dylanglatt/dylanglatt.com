Riffd

LLM-Powered Song Analyzer

If you wanted to understand how a song works — what key it’s in, what chords are playing, how the bass line moves independently from the drums — you needed a trained ear and theory background. No tool did that for you.

I built one. Pick any track: it separates every instrument, detects the key, tempo, and chords, and explains the theory in plain English. Three AI models handle the audio processing; everything runs in the browser.

Argus

Real-Time AI Conflict Intelligence

Platforms like Palantir process massive volumes of global event data in real time to surface what matters. The underlying data is public — hundreds of thousands of events logged daily. But it’s overwhelmingly noisy, and filtering it into something usable requires real infrastructure.

I built that infrastructure from scratch. A five-stage pipeline filters events using rules and AI classification, then cross-references what survives against satellite thermal imagery for independent confirmation. The output is a live map and feed of verified conflict events.
