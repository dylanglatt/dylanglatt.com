Full-stack audio platform · Live at riffdlabs.com

Riffd

Musicians can’t easily learn theory from songs they actually listen to. I needed a tool that didn’t exist — one that connects real music to real theory.

I designed and deployed a platform that takes any song, splits it into its individual parts (vocals, bass, guitar, drums), figures out the musical theory behind how it’s built, and gives you an interactive mixer to hear each part on its own — all in the browser, no downloads required.

Real-time data pipeline · Live at argusosint.vercel.app

Argus

Following geopolitical events through news is slow and editorially filtered. The most structurally significant signals get buried under alarming headlines.

I designed and deployed a real-time dashboard that pulls from a public database of 100K+ global events per day, scores each one by severity, and surfaces escalation patterns on a live map — so you can see what’s developing before it reaches the news.

LLM-powered automation · Open source

Job Scanner

Manually checking job boards doesn’t scale. Listings are scattered across multiple platforms with no unified scoring.

I designed and deployed an automated system that monitors hundreds of career pages every two hours, scores each role with an AI against a custom fit rubric, and pushes only the relevant leads directly into Notion — filtered, ranked, and ready to act on. Runs at zero cost.
