About

I'm a builder and operator based in New York. I work on products and systems — things with clear purposes, clean internals, and real uses. Most of what I build starts from a problem I ran into myself.

Right now I'm focused on Riffd, a music analysis tool for musicians that I built and run. Alongside that, I'm developing private analytical systems for market research and prediction.

I think carefully about how things are structured — both products and the code behind them. I'd rather build one thing that works well than five things that kind of work. I care about deployment, reliability, and the experience of actually using something, not just getting it to run once.

Outside of building, I'm interested in music, trading systems, and how information moves through markets and products.
