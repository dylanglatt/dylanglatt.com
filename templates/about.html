About Me

Dylan Glatt

New York, NY

I work at the intersection of data and decision-making — building analytical systems, improving the processes around them, and translating results into something anyone can act on.

My professional background is in marketing analytics. At Ipsos MMA, I supported delivery across some of the firm’s most complex engagements — including a 48-model build for General Motors that required organizing data collection, validation, and QA processes at a scale the team hadn’t handled before. Outside of that, I’ve independently built and shipped two AI-powered products: Riffd and Argus.

The through-line is the same in both: identify what the problem actually requires, build the solution, and communicate it clearly — to engineers, to executives, to whoever needs to act on it.
