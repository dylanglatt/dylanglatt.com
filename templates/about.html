About Me

Dylan Glatt

New York, NY

My background is in quantitative modeling — forecasting systems for GM and ExxonMobil, 48 vehicle-level models consolidated into a single executive dashboard, media effectiveness measured across 13 global markets. Complex work with one consistent requirement: make it simple enough for anyone to act on.

I also led the overhaul of shared marketing mix models across international offices — models that were crashing, undocumented, and causing days of back-and-forth across time zones. I standardized the structure, centralized assumptions, and introduced lightweight change logs. Crashes dropped to zero. Coordination became straightforward.

That's what I do well: understand something technical well enough to build it, and explain it clearly enough that anyone can act on it. The projects here — Riffd and Argus — apply the same approach to product development.
