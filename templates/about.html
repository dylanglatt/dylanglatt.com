About

New York, NY

I've worked on quantitative modeling for clients like GM and ExxonMobil. That ranged from building and managing 48 nameplate econometric models and condensing them into a single executive summary, to measuring and optimizing media effectiveness across 13 global CPG markets.

I scope the architecture, manage the build, and own the product decisions. I work across the full stack — from data pipelines and model integration to frontend interfaces and deployment — and I deploy fast.
