About

Dylan Glatt

New York, NY

My work has been in the operations and strategy side of analytics — with companies like GM, ExxonMobil, Fisher Investments, and Major League Soccer on how to model and optimize multi-million dollar marketing budgets. The work that matters sits close to the core of how something runs: finding where things aren't working, and doing something about it.

Most of what I've built came from needing something that didn't exist. Riffd is a music analysis tool for musicians who want to understand what's actually happening inside a track. Argus tracks global conflict events in near-real time using raw GDELT data — before it's been filtered through editorial judgment. A few private tools for market research and automated analysis round it out.
